Ninjette

DnB Girls // Safari Bass // Motion Notion // Truespin

Hailing from Calgary, Alberta, Ninjette is a natural performer. Her swift selections are a juxtaposition of deep, dark bass and playful melodies. Although she is well known for her love affair with Drum and Bass, she is also a stealthy maestro of mid-tempo sounds with a particular affection for Glitch Hop and Future Soul and Bass.
